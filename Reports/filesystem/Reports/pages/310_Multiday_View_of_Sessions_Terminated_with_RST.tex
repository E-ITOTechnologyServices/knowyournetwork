\chapter[Multiday View of Sessions Terminated with RST]{\underline{Multiday View of Sessions Terminated with RST}}
%
\begin{flushleft}
The graph shows the total amount of connections terminated by a Reset flag across the report period. A session termination by the Reset flag is unusual and can show potential misconfiguration or misbehavior on server side, within an application or a device in between the communication.
This graph provides information about single peak or recurring events.

\end{flushleft}
